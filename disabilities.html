Process of Applying for Disability Accommodations

Accommodations / Intake Process

  1. Complete Registration form. Attach documentation disability and need for accommodations. Email this to disability@columbia.edu, fax at 212-854-3448, or drop off at ODS office in Wien.
  2. ODS will review your request and either reject, approve, or ask for more info.
  3. If approved, you will be required to meet with an ODS coordinator for orientation. Policies, accommodations, and procedures will be reviewed.
  4. If rejected, you will have the chance to submit an appeal (attached to your rejection letter).
