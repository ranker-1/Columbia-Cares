Updating name and gender with Columbia

To update name:

Go to the the Registrar. This is at the Student Service Center at Kent Hall You will be asked to fill out this form: The form mentions this must be notarized. If you fill out the form in the Student Service Center, it does not need to be notarized. If you wish to have the form notarized, the UPS store near The Kraft Center has notaries typically before 12pm. They charge around $2. The USPS does NOT have a notary.

Bring copies of: A valid photo ID. A second form of ID.

Proof of use of the previous name. This can be: Court Order Credit Card University Card Marriage Certificate Birth Certificate Old Passport Bank Statement Etc
