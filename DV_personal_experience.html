My Experience

  • Went in Person SVR (Lerner 700)
  • Asked to speak to a worker as a walk-in.
  • Completed an intake. Was asked personal questions about myself and my situation.
  • Was offered support and a place of safety, if necessary. Was told all information would stay confidential.
  • Was given a Student Bill of Rights. Was required to fill out an ROI in order for information to go anywhere, including CPS.
  • Was paired to an advocate within a week. This advocate communicated with me throughout the process of getting a restraining order.
  • This involved directing me to legal resources, checking in with me emotionally, attending court with me, and even reaching out to the court on my behalf.
