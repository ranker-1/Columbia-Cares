Trans People

WARNING

IT IS CURRENTLY UNSAFE TO UPDATE YOUR SEX WITH THE PASSPORT ADMINISTRATION. DO NOT ATTEMPT TO SEND IN A PASSPORT APPLICATION UNLESS ABSOLUTELY NECESSARY. IF YOU SEND A PASSPORT APPLICATION, YOU RISK:

  • Your documents and application may be held indefinitely.
  • Your documents may be returned damaged or burnt.
  • Your documents may not be returned at all.
  • Your documents WILL be stored on a database and may be used to discriminate against you in the future, should you ever reapply for a passport.
  • Your passport WILL be sent back with the incorrect sex marker, even if you are not trying to update your sex.
  • Your passport WILL be sent back with the incorrect sex marker, even if your current passport has the correct sex marker.

The US Passport Administration has been instructed to “correct” any changes to biological sex, regardless of medical documentation or legal court orders. Information from every single application ever sent to the Administration is recorded in a database. This means if you request a new passport for whatever reason (whether it be due to losing a passport, updating legal name, updating gender, etc), the passport administration will automatically change your marker to reflect birth sex that may have indicated on previous applications. This CANNOT be corrected even if you have a birth certificate, photo ID, medical documentation, court order, etc that reflects your correct sex.

Travelling via airplane in the country is safe, even with the incorrect marker. Travelling outside the country is safe, though strongly discouraged, due to reports of discrimination on the basis of incorrect gender markers.

Current court cases on this issue are: Orr vs Trump
